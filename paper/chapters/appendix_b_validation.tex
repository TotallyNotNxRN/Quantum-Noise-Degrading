Table~\ref{tab:closed_forms} links selected exact expressions to the numerical behavior reported in the main body of the report.

\begin{table}[t]
\centering
\caption{Closed-form expressions used to validate the bundled simulations.}
\label{tab:closed_forms}
\begin{tabular}{p{4.7cm} p{8.2cm}}
\toprule
Case & Exact expression \\
\midrule
Single-qubit amplitude fidelity & $F_+^{(A)}(\gamma) = \dfrac{1 + \sqrt{1-\gamma}}{2}$ \\
Single-qubit amplitude purity & $P_+^{(A)}(\gamma) = 1 - \dfrac{\gamma}{2} + \dfrac{\gamma^2}{2}$ \\
Bell amplitude fidelity & $F_{\Phi^+}^{(A)}(\gamma) = 1 - \gamma + \dfrac{\gamma^2}{2}$ \\
Bell amplitude purity & $P_{\Phi^+}^{(A)}(\gamma) = 1 - 2\gamma + 3\gamma^2 - 2\gamma^3 + \gamma^4$ \\
GHZ phase fidelity & $F_{\mathrm{GHZ}_n}^{(P)}(\lambda) = \dfrac{1 + (1-\lambda)^n}{2}$ \\
GHZ phase purity & $P_{\mathrm{GHZ}_n}^{(P)}(\lambda) = \dfrac{1 + (1-\lambda)^{2n}}{2}$ \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

These formulas are not separate theoretical embellishments. They are derived from the same Kraus maps implemented in the simulator and therefore provide direct validation of the code path used to generate the CSV outputs.
