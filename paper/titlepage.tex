\textbf{Abstract.} This paper presents the Quantum Noise Sensitivity Mapping Engine, a reproducible density-matrix simulation study of open-system quantum-state degradation under local amplitude-damping and phase-damping channels. The work combines manually specified Kraus operators, compact numerical experiments spanning one to four qubits, and analytical cross-checks to quantify fidelity and purity loss across representative state families.
